PROTEIN FUNCTIONi
CYP2C19 catalyzes NADPH/O₂-dependent hydroxylation and epoxidation of PUFAs, primarily at the ω-1 position. It also metabolizes plant monoterpenes (e.g., converting limonene to carveol/perillyl alcohol) and is a key enzyme in the oxidation of diverse drugs, including anticonvulsants (S-mephenytoin), proton pump inhibitors (omeprazole), antifungals (fenbendazole), and antidepressants (citalopram, imipramine). This multifunctional enzyme bridges endogenous lipid metabolism and xenobiotic detoxification.
