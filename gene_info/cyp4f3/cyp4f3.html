PROTEIN FUNCTIONi
CYP4F3 is a cytochrome P450 monooxygenase that metabolizes endogenous fatty acids and oxylipins through NADPH/O₂-dependent ω-hydroxylation. It contributes to the resolution of inflammation by inactivating both pro-inflammatory and anti-inflammatory oxylipins, serving as a broad modulator of lipid mediator homeostasis.
